# Diwali Sales Analysis

This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior during the Diwali festival to identify key sales trends, high-performing segments, and business insights. The analysis helps understand customer demographics and factors influencing purchase decisions.

---

## Overview

The objective of this project is to explore and analyze Diwali sales data to uncover patterns related to customer demographics, product categories, and spending behavior. The insights can help businesses improve marketing strategies and target the right customer segments.

---

## Key Insights

* Identified top-performing customer segments based on **gender, age group, and marital status**
* Analyzed **state-wise and occupation-wise** purchasing behavior
* Determined **high-revenue product categories**
* Identified customers with **high purchasing power**
* Provided data-driven insights for targeted marketing and sales planning
